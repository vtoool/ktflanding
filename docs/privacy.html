Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2024

This site introduces FareSnap, a Chrome extension published by the KTF team. We keep this document concise so travel teams can review our approach quickly.

What we collect

  • Site analytics. We use Plausible by default. It records page views and anonymous events such as extension installs, Pro interest, and demo usage. IP addresses are never stored and no cookies are set.
  • Attribution parameters. When you visit with ?utm_source, ?utm_campaign, or ?ref, we store those values locally in your browser so they can be attached to analytics events.
  • Support contact. If you email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}, we receive the contents of your message for troubleshooting.

What we do not collect

We do not collect personal identifiers, booking data, or clipboard contents. The live demo runs entirely in your browser without network calls.

How to opt out

To disable analytics, open analytics.js and set ANALYTICS_PROVIDER to 'none'. You may also host the site without Plausible or GA4 scripts.

Data retention

We retain aggregated analytics for 13 months to understand adoption trends. Support emails are kept for one year unless you request deletion sooner.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}.
